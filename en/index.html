About Us

Your Bridge to Global Talent

European HR Services GmbH is an internationally focused recruitment consultancy based in Germany. Our mission is to connect highly skilled professionals from around the world with leading German companies - efficiently, sustainably, and with cultural alignment.

Our Mission

We believe that talent knows no borders. Our goal is to link companies with the brightest minds globally while offering candidates new career opportunities in Germany.

What Sets Us Apart

International Expertise: We have a global network and years of experience recruiting professionals from Europe, Asia, Africa, and the Americas.

Academically Qualified Team: Our consultants hold advanced degrees in HR, business, law, IT, medicine, and international relations.

Multilingual Communication: Our team speaks German, English, Spanish, French, Arabic, Russian, Hindi, and more - ensuring smooth communication with candidates and companies worldwide.

Holistic Approach: We support our clients and candidates from first contact to successful integration - including visa guidance, relocation support, and intercultural coaching.

Our Values

Trust: We build long-term relationships - with companies and candidates.

Transparency: Clear processes, open communication, and honest advice are at the heart of our work.

Diversity: We promote cultural diversity and see it as a strength for the German labor market.

Excellence: We strive for the highest quality - in consulting, selection, and support.
