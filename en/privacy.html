2. Personal Data Collection

You can generally use our website without providing personal data. If you provide us with personal data (e.g., via email), processing is based on your consent or for contract fulfillment.

3. Google Analytics 4

This website uses Google Analytics 4, a web analytics service provided by Google Ireland Limited, Gordon House, Barrow Street, Dublin 4, Ireland ("Google").

Google Analytics uses cookies and other technologies to analyze your use of the website. Processing is based exclusively on your consent in accordance with Art. 6 para. 1 lit. a GDPR. Without your consent, Google Analytics will not be loaded.

Activation Only After Consent (Consent Mode v2)

We use Google Consent Mode v2. This means Google Analytics is only activated once you have consented via the cookie banner. Before that, no cookies are set and no data is transmitted to Google.

Processed Data

  • Device information (screen resolution, operating system)
  • Browser information (type, version, language)
  • Anonymized IP address (IP anonymization is active by default in GA4)
  • Usage data (page views, clicks, time on page, bounce rate)
  • Approximate location (based on anonymized IP)

Storage Period

Event data is stored for up to 14 months by default. Cookies are automatically deleted after a maximum of 2 years.

Transfer to the USA

Data may be transferred to the USA through the service provider Google LLC (USA). This is based on the EU-US Data Privacy Framework (DPF), for which Google is certified.

Withdrawal of Consent

You can withdraw your consent at any time by clicking "Cookie Settings" in the footer and changing your selection.

For more information, please see Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Server Log Files

The hosting provider (GitHub Pages) automatically collects technical server log data (e.g., browser type, timestamp, IP address). This data is technically necessary and serves exclusively to ensure the functionality and security of the website.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies. Technically necessary cookies are set without consent. Analytics cookies (Google Analytics) are only activated with your explicit consent. You can change your cookie settings at any time in the footer under "Cookie Settings".
